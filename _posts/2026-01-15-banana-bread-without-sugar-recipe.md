Simple Banana bread recipe without added sugar.
The sweetness comes from the bananas and raisins and you can add more sweetness with honey or maple syrup if you like.

<img src="/assets/banana-bread/bread-inside.JPG" alt="Banana bread" title="Banana bread" width="500" />

Ingredients:
- 3 bananas
- 3 eggs
- Oats
- Coconut oil
- Cinnamon
- Raisins

Optional ingredients:
- Nuts
- Maple syrup
- Spices


Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius.
1. Heat the coconut oil in a small pot. When its melted, smear some of it on the inside of the form, and then put some oats in the form and shake it around so that the oats stick to the coconut oil. This will prevent the bread from sticking to the form.
1. Mash the bananas in a bowl.
1. Add the eggs to the mashed bananas and mix well.
1. Add the rest of the ingredients and mix well.
1. Pour the mixture into the form and bake for 40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

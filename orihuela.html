Types of bike lanes

No bike lane

Dangerous. Cyclists don't have their own lane.

Painted bike lane

Cyclists have their own lane, next to a road. Drivers can drive or park on the lane.

Segregated bike lane.

Cars cannot drive or park on the bike lane.

Segregated bike lane in shade

Cars cannot drive or park on the bike lane. Trees provide shade to cyclists, making the ride easier in the sun.

Things to avoid when building bike lanes

  • 90 degree turns
  • Rough edges or curbs
  • Putting bike lanes in the middle of the street

Orihuela Costa statistics

Many foreigners live in Orihuela Costa, either full time or part time. These are often people from northern Europe that are used to biking, and you can see people over 65 riding their bikes around the area.
